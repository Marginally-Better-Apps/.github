### Who are we?

As of now we are 2 university friends who decided that the apps on the app store kinda suck and we could probably do better. 

Our goal here is to take over as many of the tiny utility apps on the app store as possible and make them ad free and remove all paywalls. We want these apps to be marginally better \****roll credits\**** than the apps we are replacing and they will strive to do one thing really well.

Thank you for checking us out ❤️
